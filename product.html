AutoCoater

Description

Auto Coater is a range of advanced tablet coating systems which ensure the process of coating more efficient, reliable, flexible, user friendly and controllable. This high quality tablet coating system is designed for small volume to a large scale production size batches. Innovative science based continual developments in the all areas like tablet mixing, spraying systems, drying efficiency control, cleaning system and control system technology will guarantee a highly controlled process & ensuring the delivery of a high quality end product.
