Super Mill

The Super Mill delivers advanced grinding performance with flame-proof construction and vacuum-rated capability, engineered for high-efficiency pharmaceutical milling with seamless production line integration.

  • Basic mill is built with Flame Proof construction, ensuring safety and reliability in pharmaceutical manufacturing environments.
  • Basic design is Vacuum Rated Model, enabling safe processing of sensitive materials under controlled atmospheric conditions.
  • Can be used for milling of both dry and wet materials, providing versatility for various pharmaceutical formulations and processes.
  • Mill can be loaded manually or through Pneumatic Transfer System, offering flexibility and automation options in your production workflow.
  • Same mill can be connected with Transfer system for Blender Loading with inline milling, enabling direct integration and continuous processing.
  • Mill impeller is directly connected with Drive Shaft, and Drive shaft is connected through belt to Motor for efficient power transmission.
  • Mill features variable frequency drive for step-less drive, enabling precise control over milling speed and intensity for optimal results.
  • Very low noise level ensures a quiet and pleasant working environment for pharmaceutical manufacturing operators.
  • No need of spanner for opening of mill for cleaning, simplifying maintenance and reducing equipment downtime during batch changeover.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the Super Mill advanced compared to standard mills?

The Super Mill combines flame-proof and vacuum-rated capability with flexible loading options and inline blender integration, enabling complete production line automation with precision control.

How does inline milling improve production efficiency?

Inline milling eliminates 2-3 intermediate material transfers, saving 15-20 minutes per batch and improving product yield by 5-8% while reducing contamination risk.

Is the mill suitable for continuous production?

Yes. The Super Mill's vacuum-rated design, pneumatic transfer capability, and inline blender integration make it ideal for continuous pharmaceutical production with automated loading and processing.
